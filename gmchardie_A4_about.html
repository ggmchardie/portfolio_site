About Me

I am a computer science student/programmer with a background in both library & information services and education. I have worked as a library assistant in the public and private sectors, also as a learning support aide in the public school system.

My experiences in these different areas has motivated me to use the skills and opportunities available to me to help others.I hope to build my career based upon this

In 2019 I completed a Diploma of Information Technology at Coder Academy. I went on to start a Bachelor of Computer Science at the University of Technology Sydney. After the outbreak of COVID I transferred to Charles Sturt University. I will be graduating at the end of 2022.
