Description

I worked on LibU as part of a small team while completing my Diploma. LibU is a Ruby on Rails web app where students and teachers have access to and be able to easily find academic materials, on a wide range of subjects, without infringing on copyright laws.

Click below to view on Github

LibU


Ruby on Rails

Purpose

The goal of LibU is to provide a platform for students and teachers to upload, share and download academic materials created by their peers. This allows for materials to be covered under Creative Commons licensing and can be distributed and shared freely. In addition, LIBU is to become a community where students can share and discuss ideas with their peers and/or educators. The interface should make it easier for our users to locate relevant information by being intuitive, easy to navigate and grouping materials by subject.
